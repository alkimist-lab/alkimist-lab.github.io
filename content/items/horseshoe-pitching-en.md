# Horseshoe Pitching

Welcome to the world of fun. Horseshoe pitching is an old sport that has recently emerged as a social game.

## Game Rules

We will briefly introduce the rules as defined by the **NHPA (The National Horseshoe Pitcher's Association)**.

If you want to play by international rules, detailed instructions are available at [www.horseshoepitching.com](http://www.horseshoepitching.com).

### Local Variant

In addition to international rules, there is also a **local variant** that is popular in the Kamnik area.

## Safety

**The only rule besides having fun is safety!**

⚠️ Be careful not to injure spectators or fellow players with the horseshoe.
