# Forge Your Own Spiral! 🔥

On Culture Day, we're organizing a beginner blacksmithing workshop. You'll learn basic blacksmithing techniques and, with the help of a master, forge your first spiral in three hours. 🌀

## Event Details

- **Date:** February 8, 2026 (Culture Day)
- **Time:** 3:00 PM
- **Duration:** 3 hours
- **Location:** Sent to registered participants
- **Age Requirement:** 16+ years
- **Price:** 39 EUR per person

## Who Is This Workshop For

The workshop is suitable for men and women over 16 years old who want to learn a new craft skill. 🛠️

Previous blacksmithing experience is not required, but craft skills are welcome. 💪🙂

## What You'll Learn

- Basic blacksmithing techniques
- Working with hot iron
- Forming a spiral
- Working with blacksmithing tools

## What You'll Take Home

- Your hand-forged spiral 🌀
- Knowledge of basic blacksmithing techniques
- A unique crafting experience
