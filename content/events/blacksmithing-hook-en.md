# Create Your Own Forged Clothing Hook 🛠️🔥

At this workshop, you'll learn the basics of blacksmithing and create your own forged clothing hook to take home. 🧥✨

With the help of a blacksmith, you'll learn fundamental blacksmithing techniques such as punching hot iron, forging a point, and forming a spiral. 🔥🔩

## Event Details

- **Date:** March 15, 2026
- **Time:** 2:00 PM
- **Location:** Sent to registered participants
- **Age Requirement:** 18+ years

## Who Is This Workshop For

The workshop is suitable for men and women over 18 years old who want to learn a new craft skill. Previous blacksmithing experience is not required, but craft skills are welcome. 💪🙂

## What You'll Learn

- Punching hot iron
- Forging a point
- Forming a spiral
- Working with blacksmithing tools

## What You'll Take Home

- Your handmade forged clothing hook
- Knowledge of basic blacksmithing techniques
- A unique crafting experience
